Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy Italian pasta dish made with tender pieces of chicken, fettuccine pasta, and a rich Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta: 8 ounces of fettuccine pasta.
  • Chicken breast: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces.
  • Butter: 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter.
  • Heavy cream: 1 cup of heavy cream.
  • Parmesan cheese: 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Garlic: 2 cloves of garlic, minced.
  • Salt: Salt to taste.
  • Black pepper: Freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta until al dente, according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink and have a golden brown color.
  3. Add minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens and the cheese melts.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  6. Drain the cooked fettuccine pasta and toss it in the creamy Alfredo sauce.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese if desired.
