Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its rich and creamy sauce made with eggs, cheese, and pancetta.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti: 12 ounces of spaghetti pasta.
  • Eggs: 2 large eggs.
  • Pecorino Romano cheese: 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese.
  • Pancetta or guanciale: 4 ounces of pancetta or guanciale, diced.
  • Garlic: 2 cloves of garlic, minced.
  • Black pepper: Freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  • Salt: Salt to taste (for pasta cooking).

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente, according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, saut√© the diced pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook briefly.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta and immediately toss it with the egg and cheese mixture. The heat from the pasta will create a creamy sauce.
  5. Add the crispy pancetta or guanciale mixture and freshly ground black pepper to the pasta. Toss well to combine.
  6. Serve hot with additional grated Pecorino Romano cheese and black pepper on top.
